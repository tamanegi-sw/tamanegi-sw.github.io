Our Apps

Products we've built.

MORX

Morse code training app with paddle key, Koch method, and head copy practice. Master CW at your own pace.

Coming soon on iOS
1 / 8

Paddle Key

Tap dit/dah on a binary tree to visually learn each Morse character. Supports iambic paddle input with adjustable WPM speed.

2 / 8

Learning Menu

Choose from Koch training, head copy drills, character quizzes, word quizzes, and typing challenges — all designed to build your CW skills progressively.

3 / 8

Koch Method Training

Learn Morse by ear using the proven Koch method. New characters unlock step by step as your accuracy improves through focused practice sessions.

4 / 8

Listening Drill

Listen carefully to the Morse audio and build your ear for each character before answering. Pure audio training — the core of Koch method mastery.

5 / 8

Answer & Feedback

Pick your answer from the choices. Correct and incorrect answers are highlighted instantly so you can learn from mistakes in real time.

6 / 8

Word Typing Quiz

Decode a full word from its Morse signal and type it in. A great way to practice real-world head copy skills at your own pace.

7 / 8

Translation

Convert any text to Morse code and play it back as audio. Handy as a quick reference, for sending practice, or to share Morse messages.

8 / 8

Morse Code Table

A complete visual reference of all A–Z, 0–9, and symbol codes with dot/dash patterns at a glance. Always one tap away.
