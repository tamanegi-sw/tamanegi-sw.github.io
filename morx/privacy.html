Privacy Policy

MORX Last updated: 2026-05-01

TL;DR

MORX collects no personal data, uses no third-party tracking, and works 100% offline. Your data stays on your device.

Overview

MORX is a Morse code learning app developed by Tamanegi Software Lab. We respect your privacy and are committed to transparency about how the app handles your data.

Data Collection

MORX does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All learning progress, settings, and statistics are stored locally on your device using standard iOS mechanisms (UserDefaults) and are never sent to any server.

Third-Party Services

MORX does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Diagnostics

MORX collects diagnostic information (app events and crash data via Apple's MetricKit) locally on your device. This data is never sent automatically. You may choose to share it with us via the "Send Diagnostics Log" option in Settings. The log contains only app event timestamps and system diagnostic data — no personal information.

Feedback

If you choose to send feedback via the in-app email link, your email address and message content are handled by your device's mail application. We do not store your email address beyond what is necessary to respond to your inquiry.

Children's Privacy

MORX does not knowingly collect personal information from children. The app is suitable for all ages.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at contact@tamanegi-sw.com.
